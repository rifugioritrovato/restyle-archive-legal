Restyle Archive - Account deletion request

```

If you want to delete your Restyle Archive account and the associated data, you can request deletion by email.

How to request account deletion

Send an email to developer@rifugioritrovato.it from the same email address used to create your Restyle Archive account.

Please use the subject: Restyle Archive account deletion request

What will be deleted

We will delete your app account and the data associated with it, including cloud-stored cards and related cloud media, where applicable.

Local data and backups

Data stored only on your device, or ZIP backups exported by you, cannot be deleted remotely by Restyle Archive. You can delete local data by removing the app data from your device and deleting any backup files you exported.

Processing time

We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

Contact

For privacy and account deletion requests, contact: developer@rifugioritrovato.it

```
